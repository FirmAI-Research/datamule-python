Pursuant to Section 219 of the Iran Threat Reduction and Syria Human Rights Act of 2012 and Section 13(r) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, notice is hereby provided that Rexnord Corporation has made disclosure pursuant to such provisions in its Annual Report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2014, which was filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission on May 21, 2014.
