Ladies and Gentlemen:

Pursuant to Section 219 of the Iran Threat Reduction and Syria Human Rights Act of 2012 and Section 13(r) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, notice is hereby provided that LyondellBasell Industries N.V. has made disclosure pursuant to such provisions in its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended September 30, 2013, which was filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission on October 29, 2013. This disclosure can be found on page 55 of the Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q and is incorporated by reference herein. LyondellBasell Industries N.V. made such disclosure based on information provided by other companies that may be deemed to be under common control with LyondellBasell Industries N.V., and not because of any conduct by LyondellBasell Industries N.V.
