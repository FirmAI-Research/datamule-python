Pursuant to Section 219 of the Iran Threat Reduction and Syria Human Rights Act of 2012 and Section 13(r) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, notice is hereby provided that Ellington Residential Mortgage REIT (“EARN”) has made disclosure pursuant to such provisions in its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended March 31, 2014, which was filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission on May 13, 2014 (“Form 10-Q”).
